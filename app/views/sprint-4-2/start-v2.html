Get land and property data

Use this service to get datasets on land and property in England and Wales.

This is a new service that incorporates Commercial and Corporate Ownership Data, Overseas Companies Ownership Data and the National Polygon Service. It makes access to this data more simple and will include up to 30 more datasets in the next 3 years.

The datasets can be large and are only suitable for databases and machine reading.

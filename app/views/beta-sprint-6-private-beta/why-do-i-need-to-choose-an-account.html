Why do I need an account to download HM Land Registry data?

The data you want to see has a strict licence. This means that you can only use it in a certain way.

If you use the data outside the licence:

  • HM Land Registry will share your account details with Royal Mail
  • Royal Mail may take you to court for data misuse

When you create an account, you are telling HM Land Registry:

  • who is using this data (a person or an organisation)
  • how they have agreed to use it
  • how to contact them

When you choose your account, you are telling us who is responsible for data misuse.

HM Land Registry recommends that you:

  • read each data licence to find out how you can use the data
  • check how you can use the data with Royal Mail: example@royalmail.com

You do not need to pay anything to open an account and most of this data is also free.

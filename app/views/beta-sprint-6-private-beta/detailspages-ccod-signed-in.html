UK companies that own property in England and Wales

Download data about UK companies that own UK property. This includes the company name, type of company, registration number, address, the price paid and more. This dataset was originally named Commercial and corporate data (CCOD).

How you can use this data

Commercial and corporate ownership data is released under icensed terms of use. These terms also set out the conditions for the re-use of data (in this case, addresses) which contains third party intellectual property rights.

Property addresses contained within the commercial and corporate ownership Data are processed against Ordnance Survey’s AddressBase Premium product, which incorporates Royal Mail’s PAF® database (Address Data).
